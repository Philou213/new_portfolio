## Game Overview
**Cheryl & Donna’s Cardio Combat** is a retro beat’em up where two fitness queens dive straight into a pastel cartoon world to reclaim their time slot and put the residents of Heartland back in their place. 

## Project Context
This game was developed during the **Ubisoft Game Lab Competition 2026** in 10 weeks. The team was composed of 3 programmers from UQAC and 5 3D artists from NAD-UQAC.

## Theme and mandate
- 80s-90s 
- Multiplayer Couch game
- All Ages
- Only D-pad and 2 buttons for the controls. 

## Tools
This game was developed using **Unreal Engine 5**.
