## Game Overview  
**WonkerJam** is an arcade game featuring a collection of 10 fast-paced mini-games inspired by previous Wonderjam titles.

## Project Context  
This game was developed during **Wonderjam Fall 2025**, where it won **1st place**.

## Tools  
This game was developed using **Godot 4**.

## My Role
I worked on three mini-games:  
- *Credit Game for the Death of the Universe*  
- *À l'aide! Je suis un garde frontière et on n'arrête pas de me voler mon panneau!*  
- *Dave in the Backrooms*  

I also contributed to the game’s sound design.
